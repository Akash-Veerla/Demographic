# KON-NECT: Smart Proximity Networking

**KON-NECT** is a real-time social discovery platform that revolutionizes how you connect with the people around you. It helps you discover users based on shared interests who are currently within walking distance, fostering explicit, spontaneous, and genuine real-world interactions.

## Why KON-NECT?

We believe the best connections happen in the physical world. Rather than endlessly swiping or scrolling through digital feeds, KON-NECT anchors your social experience to your actual real-world community. Whether you're looking for a workout partner at your local gym, someone to grab a coffee with downtown, or fellow enthusiasts at an event, KON-NECT puts your immediate surroundings at your fingertips.

## Core Features

### 📍 Dynamic Local Discovery
Our interactive map shows you exactly where the energy is. Activate the 10km Discovery Mode to see users strictly within your immediate vicinity, or zoom out to the Global View to see communities connecting worldwide.

### 🎭 Intelligent Interest Matching
You aren't just a dot on a map. By explicitly defining what you care about—your hobbies, your profession, your lifestyle—KON-NECT highlights the individuals nearby who share your exact interests, making icebreakers effortless.

### 💬 Ephemeral & Private Chat
Connect instantly with zero digital footprint. Our peer-to-peer real-time chat vanishes seamlessly when you're done. No permanent chat logs, no endless scrolling histories—just immediate, private communication when it matters most.

### 🚗 Turn-by-Turn Navigation
Once you've made a connection, we help you meet up. KON-NECT features integrated real-time dynamic routing to calculate walking or driving paths directly to your new connection or shared destination.

### 🎨 Beautiful, Modern Design
Enjoy a stunning, glassmorphism-inspired interface with animated gradients, lucid iconography, and flawless dark/light mode support.

Get ready to explore your world, find your tribe, and make real-life connections with KON-NECT.
